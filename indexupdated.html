FAQ

Still Not Sure?

Answers to the most common questions about our services.

What services does Chippewa Valley Bin Cleaning offer?

We offer Garbage Bin Cleaning, Pressure Washing, Junk Removal, and Dog Poop Cleanup throughout the Chippewa Valley.

How often should I have my trash cans cleaned?

We offer monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly plans to fit your needs. For optimal hygiene, monthly cleaning is recommended to prevent bacteria, mold, and unpleasant odors.

What about winter?

We run from the beginning of April to the end of November. You will not be billed for the months that we do not operate.

How does billing work?

You will be billed on the first of each month for that month's cleaning service.

Do I need to be home for the cleaning?

No — we're hassle-free! We arrive the day after your trash pickup, so you don't even need to be home. Our specialized equipment handles everything.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the Chippewa Valley, Wisconsin — including Eau Claire, Chippewa Falls, Altoona, Lake Hallie, Menomonie, and surrounding communities.

How do I schedule a service?

Visit chippewavalleybincleaning.com to sign up through our secure digital platform, or call us at (715) 828-9411. We manage all subscriptions online for your convenience.
